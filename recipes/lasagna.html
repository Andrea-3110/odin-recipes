The layers of pasta alternate with layers of the ragu and creamy béchamel sauces, just like in the traditional lasagne recipes... but do you prefer to follow tradition to the letter or are you open to putting a little twist on it, as we’ve done here? And if you happen to end up with some leftover sausage ragu, try using it on bucatini pasta!

  • Butter 7 tbsp (100 g)

  • Fine salt to taste

    • FOR THE SAUSAGE RAGU

  • Sausage 1.75 lbs (800 g)
